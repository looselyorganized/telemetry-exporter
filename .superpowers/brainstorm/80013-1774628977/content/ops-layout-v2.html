Feedback on the "+" manage projects idea

The instinct is right — you should control what you see. But "add a folder" creates two sources of truth (the exporter auto-discovers projects from lo.yml files, and now you're also manually adding them).

Better framing: pin/unpin. All discovered projects exist in the system. The "+" becomes a drawer that shows all known projects with toggle switches. Pinned projects show as cards. Unpinned projects are hidden but still tracked. This way the exporter stays the authority for what projects exist, and the UI just controls what you see.

The mockup above shows the "+" as "Manage projects" — clicking it would open that pin/unpin drawer.
