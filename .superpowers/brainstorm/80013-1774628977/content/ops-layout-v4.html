V4 Changes

  • Left nav rail — Icon buttons: LO logo, Command (active), Activity Log, Settings. Green status dot at bottom.
  • Horizontal scrolling project cards — Cards are fixed-width (320px), scroll horizontally. Active cards are bright, idle cards are dimmed. The partial visibility of the next card hints at more content.
  • "+" card at the end — Add/remove projects. Opens a pin/unpin panel.
  • Scrolling notification ticker — Persistent bar below header that scrolls through unresolved notifications. Shows "1 of 2 ›" for paging. Warm amber background for attention without alarm.
  • Renamed "Instances" → "Agents" in project cards
  • 👋 and 🔐 icons inline — Waiting/permission indicators sit next to the agent PID line inside the Agents box. The card also gets an orange glow border for at-a-glance visibility.
  • No cache ratio in model bar — cleaner
  • Idle cards dimmed to 0.7 opacity — visual priority for active work
